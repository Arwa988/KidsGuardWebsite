Our Services

We provide smart solutions to monitor and support your child’s health anytime, anywhere.

Remote Monitoring

Allows continuous monitoring of a child’s health using smart devices. Parents can track vital signs in real time and detect health issues early without visiting the hospital.

Emergency Alerts

Automatically sends alerts to parents and caregivers when abnormal health readings are detected, helping them respond quickly in critical situations.

AI Virtual Assistant

Provides intelligent support by answering health-related questions, explaining medical data in simple language, and offering basic guidance to parents at any time.

Health Dashboard

Displays all health information in one place using clear charts and summaries. It helps parents and doctors track health trends and make better decisions.

Real-Time Chat

Real-Time Chat lets parents communicate instantly with doctors, nurses, or caregivers. They can ask questions, get advice, and receive guidance immediately.

Health Advices

Health Advices give parents useful tips and recommendations based on general medical guidelines and monitored data. They help promote healthy habits.
